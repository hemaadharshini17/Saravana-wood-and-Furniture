About JK Cake Shop

Located in the heart of Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu, JK Cake Shop has been serving delicious, affordable cakes since 2010. Owned and operated by Hema Dharshini, our mission is to bring joy to every celebration with our freshly baked goods made from the finest ingredients.

We specialize in custom cakes for all occasions - birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and corporate events. Our team of skilled bakers ensures every cake is a masterpiece!
